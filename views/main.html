What is Hygieia?

Hygieia is a web app solution that grants users easy access to health professionals in times of crisis. You can get access to these doctors by making a complaint from the website.

How does Hygieia work?

Hygieia operates by providing good, qualified and well trained doctors from different countries to listen to your complaint, observe you and give you prescription.

What kind of illness does Hygieia deal with?

Hygieia deals with illnesses non terminal diseases or illnesses. If your health is at a terminal stage, you are advised to see a doctor physically. Our doctors are however available should you want to visit them. Hygieia also offers therapy sessions.
